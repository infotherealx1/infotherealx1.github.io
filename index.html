Sudeep Tanwar's Lab

About the Lab

ST Lab is a group of researchers working with the research mentorship of Professor Sudeep Tanwar, Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Institute of Technology, Nirma University, India. ST Lab focuses on cutting-edge networking technologies especially, Sensor Networks, Smart Grid Communications, Internet of Things, and Blockchain Networking. Lab mainly focuses on simulation, analytical, & other experimental methodologies of research and publishing the results for the benefit of society. Our research is fundamental, aimed at developing and evaluating new generic blockchain & networking concepts, and application-driven, motivated by important application areas, such as transaction processing, autonomous vehicles, D2D communication, and smart grid.

Moreover, investigating how a smart society would function if blockchain technology were adopted at all levels. Making the different use cases for how blockchain technology can improve different verticals of industry 4.0.

Experts at our Lab

We employ a dedicated team of researchers for our continued and successful research and always welcome high-performance, dedicated and motivated students. Our key strength is the diversity of our team members in terms of their education and skills. ST Lab gives opportunities to graduates, scholars, computer scientists and new technology experts.

Who we are

ST Lab employs both full-time and part-time researchers with a wide range of backgrounds and technical skills. We combine technical knowledge with business and societal aspects, and we are convinced that complex social, technological and business issues can be solved with interdisciplinary approaches. We are working towards a common goal and provide our researchers with an interdisciplinary and inspiring working environment.
